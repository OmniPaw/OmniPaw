# Nhazi Project

OmniPaw ka ahaziri n'ime ọtụtụ ọkwa dị iche iche iji hụ na enwere nkewapụ siri ike n'etiti ihe nchịkwa (kernel) na sistemụ nnabata (host system).

## Nhazi Faịlụ

Project OmniPaw na-enwekarị nhazi a:

```text
├── .storage/               # Ọnọdụ mgbanwe, ndekọ, na Write-Ahead Logs (WAL)
├── execution/              # Injinia tick isi na onye nhazi nchekwa (deterministic scheduler)
├── host/                   # Ọrụ mpụga (Docker, Telemetry, TUI)
├── kernel/                 # Igwe ọnọdụ (state machine) na onye nyocha iwu (invariant checker)
├── memory/                 # Ebe nchekwa ncheta nwa oge, na-adịgide adịgide, na nkekọrịta
├── plugins/                # Ndidị ngwa ọrụ nke atọ a kwadoro
├── swarm/                  # Nhazi ọtụtụ ndị ọrụ na iwu nkwenye
└── tools/                  # Ngwa ọrụ OS ọkọlọtọ (bash, filesystem, generic)
```

## Nchebe & Nkewapụ

### Oke Nkewapụ

OmniPaw na-amanye nkewapụ n'ọkwa atọ:

1.  **Nkewapụ Ncheta (Memory Isolation)**: Onye ọrụ ọ bụla nwere `EphemeralStore` pụrụ iche. Nkwurịta okwu n'etiti ndị ọrụ ga-agarịrị site na `SharedStore` ma bụrụ nke a na-achịkwa nke ọma site na modul `Permissions`.
2.  **Nkewapụ Ọrụ (Execution Isolation)**: `TickEngine` na-ahụ na mperi nke otu onye ọrụ anaghị emebi ihe nchịkwa dum. A na-ekewapụ ndị ọrụ nwere mperi ma nwee ike itugharị ha maka nyocha.
3.  **Nkewapụ Sistemụ Nnabata (Host Isolation)**: `ToolGate` na-anapụta ọkpụkpọ mpụga niile. A na-agba ngwa ọrụ nwere nnukwu ihe ize ndụ (dịka `bash`) n'ime **Docker Containers** site na iji `DockerSandbox`.

### Inye Ikike (Permission Grants)

A ga-enyerịrị ngwa ọrụ ikike doro anya na `PermissionRegistry`. Nke a na-egbochi ndị ọrụ inyocha faịlụ sistemụ nnabata ma ọ bụ banye na netwọk na-enweghị ikike.

## Nchịkwa Ọnọdụ

Ọ bụla mgbanwe ọnọdụ na OmniPaw bụ:

- **Hash**: Iji akara SHA-256 nchekwa.
- **Dendenye**: Agbakwunyere na matrix Write-Ahead Log.
- **Nyocha**: Enwere ike itugharị ya iji gosi njedebe cryptographic.
