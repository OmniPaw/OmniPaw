# Njikwa Nchekwa (Security Invariants)

A na-achịkwa OmniPaw n'ụzọ nhazi site na **Iwu Isi 15 (Core Invariants)**. Ndị a abụghị naanị "ụkpụrụ kachasị mma"—ha bụ nyocha na-arụ ọrụ n'oge ọsọ nke ihe nchịkwa na-eme na mgbanwe ọnọdụ ọ bụla.

Ọ bụrụ na emebiri invariant, ihe nchịkwa na-ebute `Kernel Panic` ma kwụsị ọrụ ozugbo. Nke a na-egbochi onye ọrụ "na-atụ anya" (hallucinating) ime mgbanwe na-enweghị atụ na sistemụ ma ọ bụ mbanye na ncheta na-enweghị ikike.

## Usoro Amanye Invariant (The Invariant Enforcement Cycle)

`InvariantChecker` na-agba na njedebe nke ọ bụla **Tick**. Ọ na-atụnyere mgbanwe ọnọdụ a tụrụ aro na usoro mgbakọ na mwepụ ndị a:

1.  **Izi Ezi Ntuziaka (Instruction Integrity)**: Iwu a na-eme ọ̀ bụ otu na nke a debere na nsonaazụ ahụ?
2.  **Oke Ncheta (Memory Boundary)**: Onye ọrụ ọ̀ na-anwa ide na ncheta na-abụghị nke `AgentStore` ya?
3.  **Nchebe Ikike (Permission Guard)**: Onye ọrụ ọ̀ nwere akara cryptographic achọrọ maka ọrụ [ToolGate](/ig/concepts/tool-permissions) a doro anya?

## Invariants Nchekwa Kachasị Elu

| ID         | Aha                  | Egwu A Na-ebelata                                                                  |
| :--------- | :------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **INV-01** | instruction_finality | Na-egbochi prompt-injection ịgbanwe iwu a debere.                                  |
| **INV-03** | store_isolation      | Na-egbochi ncheta onye ọrụ ịfesa ebe ọzọ (belụsọ site na SharedStore).             |
| **INV-07** | tick_atomicity       | Na-ahụ na nkwụsị partial (dịka timeout) anaghị ahapụ sistemụ n'ọnọdụ mebiri emebi. |
| **INV-12** | sandbox_confinement  | Na-enyocha na ọrụ ngwa ọrụ agbanarịghị L2/L3 sandbox ya.                           |
| **INV-15** | replay_parity        | Na-ekwe nkwa na usoro REPLAY dabara kpọmkwem na trace LIVE mbụ.                    |

## Ijikwa Mmebi Invariant

Mgbe mmebi mere, ihe nchịkwa na-ewepụta ihe omume `PANIC`. Ndị mmepe nwere ike ijikwa ihe omume a iji kpalite mgbake akpaghị aka ma ọ bụ nyocha data.

```typescript
kernel.lifecycle.on("PANIC", (event) => {
  console.error(`CRITICAL: Emepirila Invariant ${event.invariantId}!`);
  // Na-akpaghị aka na-atọpụ ncheta maka nyocha
  kernel.storage.dumpState("./forensics/panic_dump.json");
});
```

### Nchọpụta Divergence

N'usoro **REPLAY**, ọ bụrụ na hash e mere ọzọ adabaghị na hash nke ndekọ mbụ, a na-ewere ya dịka `TRACE_DIVERGENCE`. Nke a bụ njikwa nchekwa kachasị elu—na-egosi na usoro ọrụ emebirighị.
